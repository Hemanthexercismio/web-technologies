Most Popular Salons in Bangalore

Bangalore has many professional salons that offer haircuts, hair styling, skin care, grooming, and beauty services. Below are three well-known salons in the city.

1. Toni & Guy

Toni & Guy is an international salon brand known for modern hair styling and professional beauty services. Many people in Bangalore visit this salon for stylish haircuts and treatments.

  • Professional hairstylists
  • Hair coloring and styling
  • Skin and beauty treatments
  • Premium salon services


2. Naturals Salon

Naturals Salon is one of the largest salon chains in India. It offers affordable beauty services for both men and women.

  • Haircuts and hair spa
  • Facial and skincare
  • Bridal makeup
  • Affordable packages


3. Green Trends

Green Trends is a popular salon brand known for modern grooming and beauty services across Bangalore.

  • Hair styling and coloring
  • Beard grooming
  • Facial treatments
  • Professional beauty experts
