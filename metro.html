NAMMA METRO - BENGALURU

Namma Metro is the rapid transit system serving the city of Bengaluru in Karnataka, India. It is operated by the Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL). The metro system helps reduce traffic congestion and provides fast transportation across the city.

Majestic Metro Station

Majestic Metro Station is the largest interchange station in the Namma Metro network. It connects both the Purple Line and Green Line and is located near Kempegowda Bus Station.

Indiranagar Metro Station

Indiranagar Metro Station is located on the Purple Line and provides easy access to shopping areas, restaurants, and business centers in Bengaluru.

Information about Metro Stations

Namma Metro stations are designed with modern facilities such as elevators, escalators, ticket vending machines, parking spaces, and security systems to ensure passenger safety and comfort.
